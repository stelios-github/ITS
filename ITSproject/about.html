Leading with Purpose and Value

Our values are not simply statements of principle or aspirations for what we endeavor to achieve—they are bonds that connect us in everything we do.

We bring bold thinking and a desire to be the best in our work in consulting, analytics, digital solutions, engineering, and cyber, and with industries ranging from defense to health to energy to international development.

Our Leadership

Jonathan Randolph

President and Chief Executive Officer

Jonathan became CEO on January 1, 2010. He is involved in all aspects of managing ITS’s national and international operations and workforce, leading efforts to set the firm’s business goals and growth strategy, build value for investors, and identify emerging trends that will affect clients.

Thalia Cobb

Area Managing Partner – Americas

Thalia is the Americas Managing Partner and Managing Partner of ITS in the US. Thalia chairs the EY Americas Operating Executive and the US Board and represents ITS in its regulatory relationships and as Executive Sponsor for inclusiveness.

Mathias Lang

Area Managing Partner – Europe

Mathias joined ITS in 1993 and was appointed to this position in 2013. Prior to this appointment, Mathias served as the Regional Managing Partner of the Financial Services Organization (FSO) for ITS and was a member of the Board of Directors and Chief Administrative Officer of ITS.
